# Changelog

## structest 0.1.0

- Initial release.
- Implements
  [`test_t0()`](https://felipelfv.github.io/structest/reference/test_t0.md):
  reliability-dependent test of structural interpretation (Section 3.2
  of VanderWeele & Vansteelandt, 2022).
- Implements
  [`test_t1()`](https://felipelfv.github.io/structest/reference/test_t1.md):
  reliability-independent test of structural interpretation (Section 3.3
  of VanderWeele & Vansteelandt, 2022).
- Implements
  [`estimate_reliability()`](https://felipelfv.github.io/structest/reference/estimate_reliability.md):
  estimation of reliability coefficients via quasi-Poisson GLM on
  pairwise covariances (Section 3.1).
